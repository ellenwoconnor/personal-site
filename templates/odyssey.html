Overview: Brain Odyssey is a data visualization webapp that allows users to explore neuroscience literature by interacting with a 3D brain. Querying a set of over 10,000 scientific articles, Brain Odyssey makes it possible to explore associations between words/topics and locations in the human brain -- making current findings accessible to the public while abstracting away the technical jargon internal to the research community.

Stay tuned! This project will be deployed soon. In the meantime, feel free to check it out on github.
